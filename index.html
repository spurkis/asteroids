    Space Shooter using HTML5 Canvas + Javascript

    Controls:

    • up/i: accelerate forward
    • down/k: accelerate backward
    • left/j: spin ccw
    • right/l: spin cw
    • space: shoot
    • w: switch weapon

    Using Internet Explorer? Try pressing "F12" before you click on a level, then pressing "F12" again.

    Note that this will perform poorly (if at all) on most mobile devices! Yes, that includes iPads & Android tablets, as well as phones.

    Features:

    • gravity
    • planets
    • land on planets
    • enemy ship
    • fired weapons are shootable

    Not yet Features:

    • sound
    • fancy graphics
    • lots more...
